# Circle Calculus Paper Roadmap

## Volume I — Finite Foundations

1. **Finite Circles, Rotations, and Coils**  
   Defines C_n, nodes, rotations, coils, closure, period, prime full-coil behavior.

2. **Winding, Lifted Circles, and Natural Numbers**  
   Adds winding/residue pairs and models natural-number arithmetic as lifted circular motion.

3. **Integers, Orientation, and Reversible Motion**  
   Adds clockwise/counterclockwise orientation and integer arithmetic.

4. **Multiplication, Scaling, Factors, and Prime Coils**  
   Treats multiplication as repeated rotation and scaling maps; proves invertibility iff coprime.

5. **Number Provenance Spaces**  
   Treats a number as the family of certified constructions, decompositions, partitions, factors, and coils.

## Volume II — Proof, Syntax, Glyphs, and Gödel

6. **Glyph Calculus I: Diagrams That Compile to Proof Terms**  
   Defines visual syntax and compilation to proof terms.

7. **Circle Proof Theory I**  
   Defines formal proof rules.

8. **Normal Forms, Equality, and Canonicalization**  
   Defines rewrite rules, normal forms, canonical hashes.

9. **Gödel Coils**  
   Encodes syntax/proofs/glyphs as numbers/circular addresses. Explicitly states incompleteness limits.

10. **Soundness of Finite Circle Proofs**  
    Proves Circle finite-core proofs translate to true theorems of standard modular arithmetic.

11. **Fragment Completeness for the Finite Core**  
    Proves completeness for bounded rotation/orbit/closure fragments, not global completeness.
